The program begins with the Player as a deer. Several powerups are placed in a script at start up.

The powerups disappear and reappear after delays.

The purple powerups turn the Player into a penguin.

The green powerups turn the Player into a bear.

Eventually a gorilla spawns, who then goes for the powerups as well. The powerups do not change the gorilla's form.
